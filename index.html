Welcome to our office!!! We'd like to make friends with you and invite you to join our family. Let's have fun and make progress together. For more information about us, please look at the Introduction page.

Our office is called "Marine Geology Office 201". The office is located in the Ocean Academic building, which is in opposite of the YuanZheng hotel. In this office, 11 students study and work everday, including 1 postdoctor, 5 doctors and 5 masters. They all are students of Prof ChunFeng Li and Prof Yingmin Wang. The main research interests of us are Marine Geology, Geophysics and Sedimentary.
